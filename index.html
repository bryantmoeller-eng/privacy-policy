Up North Lake Finder – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Information We Collect

Up North Lake Finder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Location: The app requests access to your device's approximate location solely to center the map near you. Your location is used on-device only and is never sent to any server or stored by us.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Maps to render map tiles. Google's own privacy policy governs those requests: policies.google.com/privacy.

Lake survey data is sourced from the Minnesota DNR LakeFinder public dataset and is bundled within the app. No requests are made to DNR servers at runtime.

Data Retention

We do not collect or retain any user data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
